Our Story

Founded in 2020, Brew Haven began as a small coffee cart in Colombo. What started as a passion project between two college friends quickly grew into Sri Lanka's most beloved artisanal coffee brand.

Today, we operate 10+ locations across the country while maintaining our commitment to ethically sourced beans and handcrafted quality.
